How to Play Super Panda

🎮 Controls

  • ⬅️ Move Left: Arrow Left / A
  • ➡️ Move Right: Arrow Right / D
  • ⬆️ Jump: Space / W / Arrow Up

🎯 Objectives

  • 🚩 Reach the flag pole to complete level
  • 🎋 Collect bamboo for points
  • 🥟 Eat buns to grow bigger
  • ⭐ Collect stars for invincibility

⚔️ Combat

  • 🐭 Jump on mice to defeat them
  • ❤️ Don't touch mice bodies
  • ⚠️ Avoid falling into pits

🏆 Game Features

Experience classic platform gaming with adorable panda graphics, collect items, defeat enemies, and complete challenging levels!
